About Us

Cashify was developed by a passionate team of students from PUP with the goal of making money management simpler and more accessible. Our easy-to-use coin and bill scanner quickly identifies currency and speaks the value aloud, offering a perfect solution for those with low vision or anyone seeking a more convenient way to handle cash. Driven by innovation and accessibility, we aim to empower users with a reliable tool to confidently manage their money anytime, anywhere.
